About us

Welcome to eFootball Edit Community, the ultimate destination for all football lovers and card editing enthusiasts! We are a community-driven platform that brings together individuals with a shared passion for football and graphic design.

Our platform is based on the popular messaging app, WhatsApp, making it easy and convenient for members to connect and share their work. This enables them to collaborate, share ideas, and receive feedback from fellow members.

At eFootball Edit Community, we provide a unique and exciting experience for our members by hosting challenges with real cash or asset prizes. These challenges not only allow our members to showcase their skills but also give them the opportunity to win exciting rewards.

We believe in promoting healthy competition and encouraging our members to continuously improve their skills. By doing so, we can create a dynamic and creative community where members can grow and develop together.

Rules

Mild warnings are given to members who engage in disruptive behavior, such as sending repetitive texts (spam), using foul language, sending inappropriate stickers, and provoking chaos, among other things.

Medium warnings are given to members who commit acts such as stealing other members' work, promoting personal interests (excluding editing), and other similar actions.

Severe warnings are given to members who exhibit poor behavior, such as making jokes about religion and politics, sharing inappropriate content, insulting individuals or groups, racism, and other similar actions.
